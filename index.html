The Setup

Who is this actually for?

50+ total attendees later, one question keeps coming up:
"Okay, but where do I even start?" — Let's answer that.

Ideal Attendee
You drink coffee daily but couldn't explain why your cup tastes burnt. You're curious, not a snob — and you've never heard of a pourover outside a menu.
1
Also You
You've attended one of my tastings and thought "I wish I knew more." Or you've never come but always wanted to. Either way — you belong here.
2
Not Ideal
If you already know your grind ratios and dial in espresso before 7am, you might find this a bit remedial. We still love you. Please come anyway and be patient.
3
The Goal
Leave knowing exactly how to make a great cup at home — and why it works. No jargon, no gatekeeping, just really good coffee.
4
What to Expect

The details.

We'll cover bean origins & roast levels, why the grinder matters more than the machine, and troubleshooting a bad cup. We'll also have a live brewing demo, and finish off with a curated tasting.
